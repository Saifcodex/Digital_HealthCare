Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule an appointment with a doctor?

You can schedule an appointment with a doctor through our website by selecting your desired specialty, preferred location, and available time slot. Simply follow the prompts to complete the booking process

What medical services do you offer ?

We offer a wide range of medical services, including general consultations, specialty treatments, diagnostic tests, preventive care, rehabilitation therapies, mental health support, and more. Visit our Services page for a comprehensive list of offerings.

How can I purchase medicine and medical equipment from your website?

You can purchase prescribed medicine and medical equipment directly through our website's pharmacy and store sections. Browse our inventory, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout for a seamless purchasing experience

What are the steps to find a blood donor through your platform?

To find a blood donor, simply navigate to our Blood Donation section and provide details such as blood type, location, and urgency. Our platform will match you with compatible donors in your area, facilitating the donation process.

How do I book an ICU bed in case of an emergency ?

In case of an emergency requiring ICU admission, please contact our emergency hotline immediately. Our dedicated team will assist you in booking an available ICU bed at the nearest hospital for prompt medical attention.

How can I provide feedback or report an issue with your services ?

We value your feedback and encourage you to reach out to us through our Contact page or customer support email. Your input helps us improve our services and ensure a positive experience for all users.
